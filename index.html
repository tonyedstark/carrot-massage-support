Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does the app work?

Carrot Massage uses your phone's built-in vibration motor to create massage patterns. Simply:

  • Open the Carrot Massage app
  • Choose your preferred vibration pattern
  • Select background music (optional)
  • Place the phone on the area you want to massage
  • Press start and enjoy

What vibration patterns are available?

The app offers multiple vibration patterns including continuous, pulsing, wave, and custom patterns. You can adjust the intensity and duration to suit your preferences.

Is the app free?

Yes! Carrot Massage is completely free to download and use. The app is supported by non-intrusive advertisements.

Does the app collect my personal data?

No. Carrot Massage does NOT collect, store, or share any personal user data. The app only integrates standard advertising networks. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Will this drain my battery?

The vibration feature does use battery power. For longer sessions, we recommend keeping your phone plugged in or ensuring you have sufficient battery charge.

Can I use this for actual massage therapy?

This app is designed for relaxation and light massage purposes only. It is not intended to replace professional medical massage therapy or treatment for any medical conditions.
